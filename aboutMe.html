I am a Bay-Area native and UC Santa Barbara graduate with a degree in Accounting and a professional background as a Recruitment Manager for accounting and finance roles in private industry. I have three complete web application projects, two of which are built with (unique) full-stacks, and only one reason for becoming a software engineer: money… Only joking!

The real reason, distilled to a single sentence: I want to be on a team that cares about their product. Technologies and skills that I have demonstrated experience in:

  • Eventlite - (Eventbrite clone with Ruby on Rails and Postgres DB backend) harnessed the unidirectional state management of Redux to simplify React components and directly manage data for form inputs and form submissions
  • Key-Wi - (platform for users to share their custom keybinds for different video games) utilized the MERN stack (Express, Mongoose, React, Redux, Node, CSS3, Webpack, MongoDB) and as backend lead I implemented nested objects within binding objects in the DB collection per Mongo DB’s document-based system structure.
  • BlockWheels - (side scroller car game), I designed a dynamic 6-point hit box (even while rotating) for the car by incorporating JavaScript built-in trigonometric functions to track all four corners and 2 sides relative to cars central point and angle to ground, consistently outperforming canvas’ built-in collision function

As a former recruiter, I focused on improving my ability to communicate efficiently. As a developer, I know I would save your team time by knowing what questions I need to ask up front, and what details I need to relay when receiving tasks or asking for help. As both, I am confident that I am an ideal candidate for your software development team.
